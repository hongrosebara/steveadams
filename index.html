Journey Through The Mirror

From wild adventure to the ecosphere of a coma

Throughout this true story, Steven has seamlessly merged many worlds, from wild adventure to the ecosphere of a coma, from the high drama of life lived on a knife’s edge, to the overcoming of adversity in the form of total blindness. During the course of these pages it is impossible to guess which way the story will turn, and I was kept on the edge of my seat in a constant rollercoaster of psychology, intellect, daring, beautiful imagery, adventure and deep thought processes, to name a few.

He has taken me on a journey into domains I had no idea existed and has shown me the lighter side of life, in situations appearing too dark to be laughed at. He has given me a totally different perspective on how to move forward when life seems difficult to face. Above all, it is entertainment at its best.

Journey Through The Mirror has a stand alone style and it is fair to say, it is unique.

Jack Hondros, Editor.

About The Author

Steve Adams

Steve Adams was born in Albany, Western Australia in 1968. His life has been filled with adventure both in Australia and all over the world. Being blinded in an accident 14 years ago, changed the course of his life and he now lives quietly in the countryside of WA, with his wife Diana and two children.
